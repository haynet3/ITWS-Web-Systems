What is Gamma Nu Eta? Glad you asked.

Gamma Nu Eta was established as the National Information Technology Honor Society to recognize and promote leadership and excellence in the study of IT. We provide academic services such as tutoring and run career-related events such as the Company Expose. To find out more, visit our Actvities page.
